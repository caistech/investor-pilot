## Why v3 exists

v1 framed the offering as the tokenised GREH Fund. v2 split into three tracks (parent equity / project SPVs / tokenised fund). v2.1 incorporated the senior-lender Finance Submissions V10. **v3 inverts the InvestorPilot channel target:**

- **Old (v2.1):** InvestorPilot targets financial advisors placing $125K wholesale equity/debt tickets into project SPVs.
- **New (v3):** InvestorPilot targets HNW direct lenders + family office private debt allocators writing $1M-$5M senior secured cheques into the Branscombe + Seafields facilities.

**Why the change:** Stamford Capital + Front Financial were engaged to place the combined ~$18.7M senior facility. That broker process has stalled. InvestorPilot is now the **parallel direct-to-lender process** replacing the broker channel.

The $125K wholesale equity layer is being sourced via Uwe's own network and is **out of scope for InvestorPilot's outreach platform**.

## Part 4 — Lender ICP (replaces v2.1 advisor ICP entirely)

### Decision-maker profile

| Field | Best fit |
|---|---|
| **Decision-maker** | Family office principal or CIO with personal allocation authority; HNW individual who personally lends into property development; principal of a private debt vehicle |
| **Capital per cheque** | $1M-$5M typical; some up to $10M+ |
| **Asset class focus** | Private debt — specifically Australian property and development debt |
| **Risk position** | Comfortable with first-mortgage senior secured at 65-75% LVR on development land or construction |
| **Return target** | 8-11% p.a. coupon, contracted-term debt — NOT equity-style upside |
| **Decision cadence** | Weeks, not months. Capable of moving on credit decisions without committee delay. |
| **Geography** | Australia primarily. Singapore + Hong Kong family offices with AU property allocations in scope. |
| **Existing track record** | Has lent into ≥1 Australian property development facility in the past 36 months — **strongest single predictor** |
| **Reachability** | Findable on LinkedIn and via professional networks. Visible enough to enrich with verified email. |

### Accept these roles/titles

- Family Office Principal, Founder, Director, or CIO
- Head of Private Debt / Head of Credit / Head of Real Estate Debt at family-office-backed private debt vehicle
- Principal or Managing Partner of a private credit fund that allocates to property development
- HNW individual described publicly as a property investor, developer, or private lender
- Director or Trustee of a SMSF with documented private debt allocations
- Founder or Principal of a property syndication business that lends as well as places equity

### Reject these roles/titles

- Retail bank credit officers (wrong channel, wrong approval pathway)
- Mortgage brokers (we already have a stalled broker process)
- Equity-only family offices that do not allocate to debt
- Tech / venture-focused family offices
- Public REIT managers (wrong return profile)
- Pure listed-equity advisors
- **Generic financial advisors placing retail client money** (wrong product — note this is reversed from v2.1)

### Firm/vehicle types we accept

- Single-family offices with declared private debt / real estate debt allocations
- Multi-family offices with private debt mandates
- Private credit funds focused on Australian property development debt — **sub-$200M AUM tier** (Qualitas / MaxCap tier is too large; would expect whole-of-deal)
- Private debt vehicles within boutique investment houses
- Wholesale property syndicators that occasionally co-lend
- Self-managed lending vehicles owned by HNW developers who lend on the side

### Firm/vehicle types we reject

- Large institutional debt funds (>$1B AUM) — too big for our facility sizes
- Bank-owned platforms — credit committee timelines incompatible with parallel-process posture
- Retail mortgage trusts and listed mortgage funds
- Equity-only family offices
- Pure private equity funds

### Geographic priority (DELIBERATE departure from v2.1)

| Geography | Priority | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Sydney** | HIGHEST | Greatest concentration of family offices with private debt mandates |
| **Melbourne** | HIGH | Strong family office community, several known private debt allocators |
| **Singapore (with AU property allocation)** | HIGH | Significant FO presence; many already lend into AU development |
| Brisbane / Perth | MEDIUM | Smaller pool but high-value when located |
| Hong Kong (with AU property allocation) | MEDIUM | Similar profile to Singapore, slightly smaller universe |
| **TAS / Geraldton (project locales)** | **LOW** | Local angle does not apply for senior debt — lenders are not geographically tethered to project site |

**This is the opposite of v2.1's geographic priority** (which prioritised TAS/WA advisors for project-locality fit). Credit teams lend into projects they never visit. Centre of gravity is Sydney + Melbourne + Singapore.

## Part 5 — Re-Weighted Scoring Rubric

Schema field names (`audience_overlap_score`, `complementarity_score`, `partner_readiness_score`, `reachability_score`, `strategic_leverage_score`) are retained to avoid migration risk. **The SEMANTICS each field captures is updated** for the lender ICP. Documented as known tech debt — proper rename in a future migration.

| Schema field | Lender-ICP semantic | Weight | What 10/10 looks like |
|---|---|---|---|
| `audience_overlap_score` | **Capital available + ticket fit** | **25%** | Writes $2-5M private debt cheques regularly; capacity for $5M+ documented |
| `complementarity_score` | **Asset class focus (AU property dev debt)** | **25%** | Explicitly markets / publicly engages on Australian property development debt; recent comment / posts / co-invests visible |
| `strategic_leverage_score` | **Track record — has lent into AU property dev debt in past 36mo** | **25%** | Public evidence of ≥1 AU property dev debt position in last 36mo (LinkedIn post, fund report, news mention) |
| `partner_readiness_score` | **Decision authority + cadence** | **15%** | Personal allocation authority; can decide in weeks; no slow committee gating |
| `reachability_score` | **Reachability + geographic concentration fit** | **10%** | Sydney / Melbourne / Singapore based; high LinkedIn visibility; findable email |

**Total: 100%.** Compare to v2.1 (Client Quality 30% / Alt-History 30% / Reachability 20% / Regulatory 15% / Geographic 5%) — note Geographic dropped from 5% to 10% but with different meaning, Regulatory removed entirely (lenders need to be reachable and active, not AFSL-regulated themselves), Reachability dropped from 20% to 10% (we'd rather have the right credit profile in the wrong city than vice versa).

### "10/10 lender" description (for LLM discovery prompt)

> A 10/10 lender for F2K's senior debt facilities is a family office principal, CIO, or HNW private lender based in Sydney, Melbourne, or Singapore. They personally write $2-5M cheques into private debt, with documented exposure to Australian property and development debt in the past 36 months. They are comfortable with first-mortgage senior secured positions at 65-75% LVR on development land or construction. They expect 8-11% p.a. coupon-style returns on contracted-term debt. They can decide in weeks without committee delay. They are findable on LinkedIn with a verifiable work email. Title is Family Office Principal/Director/CIO, Head of Private Debt/Credit/Real Estate Debt at a family-office-backed vehicle, Principal/Managing Partner of a sub-$200M private credit fund focused on AU property, or HNW individual publicly described as property investor/developer/private lender.
